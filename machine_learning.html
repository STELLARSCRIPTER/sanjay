Machine Learning for Climate Change

Description: This project involves leveraging machine learning algorithms to predict and analyze climate change trends. It encompasses data collection from various sources, preprocessing, model training, and evaluation to provide actionable insights into future climate patterns and the impact of different variables.

Advantages

  • Predicts future climate trends using historical data, helping to anticipate and prepare for future changes.
  • Provides insights into the impact of various environmental and human factors on climate change.
  • Facilitates data-driven decision-making for environmental policy and strategies, contributing to better climate action plans.

Tools Used

  • Python: Utilized for implementing machine learning models (Scikit-learn, TensorFlow) and data manipulation.
  • Jupyter Notebook: Used for interactive development and visualization of data and model performance.
  • Matplotlib and Seaborn: Employed for creating comprehensive data visualizations to interpret and present results effectively.

Time Taken

Approximately 4 weeks, including time for data collection, model development, and detailed analysis.
