Stability of Black Holes

Description: An in-depth analysis of black hole stability, exploring the dynamics and implications of gravitational forces. This project involves mathematical modeling and simulations to understand the conditions under which black holes remain stable or undergo significant changes.

Advantages

  • Provides insights into the fundamental aspects of black hole physics.
  • Enhances understanding of gravitational dynamics and cosmic phenomena.
  • Contributes to theoretical astrophysics and cosmology research.

Tools Used

  • Mathematical Modeling Software (e.g., MATLAB, Mathematica)
  • Simulation Tools (e.g., Python for numerical simulations)
  • Data Analysis Tools (e.g., Python, R)

Time Taken

Approximately 8 weeks, including literature review, model development, simulations, and analysis.
